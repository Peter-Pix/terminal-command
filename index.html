Why Master the Terminal?

🚀 Speed & Efficiency

Executing complex tasks with a single line is vastly faster than navigating multiple GUI menus. Think "pipelining" and automation.

🤖 Automation Power

Scripts (Bash, PowerShell) allow you to automate repetitive tasks—saving hours every week.

🌐 Remote Management

SSH access lets you manage servers anywhere in the world directly from your command line.

💡 Deeper Understanding

Using the terminal forces you to understand the underlying structure of your operating system.
